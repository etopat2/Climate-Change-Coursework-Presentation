Climate Change Overview

The defining challenge of our century
1.45°C

above pre-industrial levels
2023 — hottest year on record (WMO, 2024)

Key Drivers

  • Fossil fuel combustion
  • Deforestation
  • Industrialization
  • Unsustainable agriculture

Consequences

  • Rising global temperatures
  • Extreme weather (floods, droughts)
  • Biodiversity loss
  • Food insecurity

Paris Agreement Threshold

1.5°C — urgent action needed to avoid severe, irreversible impacts.

Climate Change Impacts on IT

Vulnerabilities in the digital ecosystem

Increased Energy Demand

Rising temperatures increase cooling costs for data centres.

Physical Damage

Floods, storms, wildfires threaten critical infrastructure.

E-waste

Shortened device lifecycles increase pollution.

Cybersecurity Risks

Attacks during emergencies, data breaches, infrastructure sabotage.

Supply Chain Disruptions

Semiconductor manufacturing affected by water scarcity & droughts.
